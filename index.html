    ABOUT REFRESKO 2025

    Refresko 2025, the flagship fest of Supreme Knowledge Foundation Group of Institutions, is a grand celebration where technology and culture come together. This year, our theme is Srijan, which means creation, symbolizing innovation, artistic expression, and new beginnings. The event features TechCraze, a dynamic tech fest with gaming battles, coding challenges, tech quizzes, typing competitions, ideation contests, a Rubikâ€™s Cube challenge, a photography competition, and hands-on innovation. Alongside the tech excitement, the Cultural Fest brings a vibrant mix of music and dance performances, fashion shows, art exhibitions, and a variety of food stalls, creating an atmosphere of creativity and celebration. Whether you are here to compete, innovate, perform, or simply experience the energy, Refresko 2025 Srijan promises a day filled with excitement, discovery, and unforgettable memories at Supreme Knowledge Foundation.
